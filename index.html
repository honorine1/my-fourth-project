ABOUT US

M&H's Pizzeria is a family owned local pizzeria serving the best pizza in town! The pizzeria was established in 2018 in Rwamagana and in jan/2019 moved to its current location in kigali/kimironko city. We use the highest quality ingredients from locally sourced vendors with each pizza crafted to your liking!
